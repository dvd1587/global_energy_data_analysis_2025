# NYC Global Energy Data Exploration & Analysis - 2025 (Ember Energy)

### [Kaggle Demonstration](https://www.kaggle.com/code/dvd1587/monthly-energy-data-analysis-ember-energy)

<h2>Description</h2>
This project is an exploratory data analysis of the global monthly energy dataset (up until last year 2025) from Ember Energy downloaded on January 2026. The data is analysed for electricity trends, energy transition, country-level breakdown, fuel mix, regional comparison, carbon intensity, seasonality of solar and wind energy, and YoY growth, etc.
<br/>


<h2>Languages and Utilities Used</h2>

- <b>Python</b>
